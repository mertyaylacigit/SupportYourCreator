How It Works

Your supporters get rewarded for playing your map!

1

Play the Fortnite Creative Map

The Tracker tracks the time played on the map for all players.

2

Press Time Tracker

After pressing the Time Tracker button, a message about the played time will appear.

3

Take Image

Take an image of that message that is a proof how long the player played the map.

4

Upload to Discord

Upload this proof to the chat with the SupportYourCreator Bot in the discord server of the content creator.

5

Participate in the Giveaway

After the bot approves the proof the player gets access to the giveaway where the chance to win increase the higher the played time is.
