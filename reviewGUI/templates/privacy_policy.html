Privacy Policy

We process your Epic Games account username and link it to your Discord account to complete the verification process within our SupportYourCreator project. This data is used solely for its intended purpose and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data at any time. Your data belongs to you, and you have full control over its management and deletion.

Contact

If you have any questions about data protection or wish to exercise your rights under the GDPR, you can contact us at any time by email at privacy@supportyourcreator.com.
